Singapore Public Transport Dashboard

Created a dashboard to analyze MRT and bus ridership trends across Singapore using public LTA data. Built filters by station, year, and transport mode, allowing stakeholders to view underused and peak locations. Presented insights on commuter behavior, seasonal ridership patterns, and transport planning opportunities.

Housing Price Dashboard

Cleaned and transformed data from Singapore’s public datasets using Excel and Tableau Prep. Designed an interactive Tableau dashboard visualizing HDB resale flat prices across towns, flat types, and years. Enabled filters for town, flat type, and year, helping users identify price trends and high-demand areas.

Restaurant Dashboard

This report analyzes the annual sales performance, highlighting trends by month, day, product, and hour. The insights help identify the strongest revenue drivers and pinpoint opportunities for improvement and targeted growth.
